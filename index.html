About DJ

images/dj-photo.jpg

Revenue Cycle Knowledge Bank by DJ is a practical learning platform focused on US Healthcare Revenue Cycle Management, operational excellence, analytics, automation, Lean Six Sigma, AR management, denial prevention and leadership development.

The goal of this platform is to simplify complex RCM concepts and convert real-world healthcare operations experience into useful learning content for professionals, team leaders, managers and organizations looking to strengthen revenue cycle capabilities.

This website will also serve as a knowledge hub where LinkedIn posts, RCM insights, blog articles, templates and training resources can be organized in one place.
